# Last-Minute Summary έΑΦ Complex Numbers

## Key formulas
$(\\cos\\theta + i\\sin\\theta)^n = \\cos n\\theta + i\\sin n\\theta$

$\\cos\\theta = \\frac{z+z^{-1}}{2}$, $\\sin\\theta = \\frac{z-z^{-1}}{2i}$
$z^n + z^{-n} = 2\\cos n\\theta$, $z^n - z^{-n} = 2i\\sin n\\theta$

$n$th roots of unity: $z_k = e^{2\\pi i k/n}$ for $k = 0,\\ldots,n-1$
Sum of all roots $= 0$

## C + iS method
1. Form $C + iS = \\sum e^{i\\theta_r}$
2. Sum geometric series
3. Take real part for $C$, imaginary for $S$

## Common series results
$\\sum_{r=0}^{n-1} \\cos(2r+1)\\theta = \\frac{\\sin 2n\\theta}{2\\sin\\theta}$
$\\sum_{r=0}^{n-1} \\sin(2r+1)\\theta = \\frac{1 - \\cos 2n\\theta}{2\\sin\\theta}$
