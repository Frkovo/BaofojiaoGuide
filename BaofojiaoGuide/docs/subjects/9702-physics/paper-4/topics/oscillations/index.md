# Oscillations

## 考纲要求

- 17.1 Simple harmonic oscillations
  - Use displacement, amplitude, period, frequency, angular frequency, phase difference
  - SHM: $a \propto -x$ (acceleration proportional to displacement, opposite direction)
  - Use $a = -\omega^2 x$ and $x = x_0 \sin \omega t$
  - Use $v = v_0 \cos \omega t$ and $v = \pm \omega \sqrt{x_0^2 - x^2}$
  - Analyse graphs of $x$, $v$, $a$ for SHM
- 17.2 Energy in SHM
  - Describe KE-PE interchange during SHM
  - Use $E = \frac{1}{2} m \omega^2 x_0^2$ for total energy
- 17.3 Damped and forced oscillations, resonance
  - Resistive force causes damping
  - Light, critical, heavy damping — sketch $x$-$t$ graphs
  - Resonance: max amplitude when driving frequency = natural frequency

## 常见题型

- 定义题：State what is meant by simple harmonic motion
- 计算题：求频率、最大速度、最大加速度、能量等
- 作图题：Sketch $x$-$t$, $v$-$t$, $a$-$t$ 图或 $v$-$x$ 图
- 解释题：Explain damping / resonance / energy interchange

## 核心公式

$$a = -\omega^2 x$$

$$x = x_0 \sin \omega t$$

$$v = v_0 \cos \omega t = \pm \omega \sqrt{x_0^2 - x^2}$$

$$v_0 = \omega x_0$$

$$\omega = 2\pi f = \frac{2\pi}{T}$$

$$E = \frac{1}{2} m\omega^2 x_0^2$$

## 常见错误

- 位移最大时速度也为零（不是最大）
- 加速度始终指向平衡位置（$a = -\omega^2 x$）
- 混淆 $\omega$ 和 $f$：$\omega = 2\pi f$
- 共振条件：驱动力频率 = 系统自然频率
