# Thermodynamics

## 考纲要求

- 16.1 Internal energy
  - Understand that internal energy is determined by the state of the system and expressed as the sum of random kinetic and potential energies of molecules
  - Relate a rise in temperature to an increase in internal energy
- 16.2 The first law of thermodynamics
  - Use $W = p\Delta V$ for work done when volume changes at constant pressure, distinguishing work done by the gas vs work done on the gas
  - Use $\Delta U = q + W$ (increase in internal energy = heating of system + work done on system)

## 常见题型

- 定义题：State what is meant by internal energy / first law
- 计算题：$\Delta U = q + W$ 表格填空或计算
- 解释题：Use kinetic theory to explain temperature change during expansion/compression
- 推导题：Show that the work done during an isobaric change is $p\Delta V$

## 核心公式

$$W = p\Delta V$$

$$\Delta U = q + W$$

$$U = \sum E_k + \sum E_p$$

## 常见错误

- $\Delta U = q + W$ 符号混淆：系统对外做功时 $W$ 为负
- $W = p\Delta V$ 中错用 $\Delta V$ 的符号（膨胀 $\Delta V > 0$, $W$ 为负）
- 混淆 $q$ 和 $W$ 的"正"的含义：$+q$ 是系统吸热，$+W$ 是对系统做功
- 忘记内能包括动能和势能两部分（对理想气体，势能为零）
