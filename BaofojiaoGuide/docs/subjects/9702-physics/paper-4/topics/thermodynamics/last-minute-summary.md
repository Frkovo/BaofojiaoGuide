# Thermodynamics — 考前速记

## 核心公式

$$W = p\Delta V$$

$$\Delta U = q + W$$

## 符号速查表

| 过程 | $\Delta U$ | $q$ | $W$ |
|------|------------|-----|-----|
| 等体 (isochoric) | $\Delta U = q$ | $\Delta U$ | 0 |
| 等压 (isobaric) | $\Delta U = q - p\Delta V$ | $\Delta U + p\Delta V$ | $-p\Delta V$ |
| 等温 (isothermal) — 理想气体 | 0 | $-W$ | $W$ |
| 绝热 (adiabatic) | $W$ | 0 | $\Delta U$ |
| 循环 (cyclic) | 0 | $-W$ | $W$ |

## 考前 checklist

- [ ] 记住内能 = 分子随机动能 + 势能的总和
- [ ] 对理想气体，$U$ 只与 $T$ 有关
- [ ] $\Delta U = q + W$ 中 $W$ 是 work done **on** the system
- [ ] 气体膨胀（$\Delta V > 0$）：$W$ 为负
- [ ] 气体压缩（$\Delta V < 0$）：$W$ 为正
- [ ] $p$-$V$ 图面积 = 功的大小
- [ ] 循环过程 $\Delta U = 0$
- [ ] 等体过程 $W = 0$

## 关键句

> Internal energy = sum of random distribution of kinetic and potential energies of molecules.

> $\Delta U = q + W$: increase in internal energy = heating of system (to system) + work done on system.
