# Electric Fields — MS 模式

## 定义题模式

| 术语 | 分值 | 标准答案 |
|------|------|---------|
| electric field | B1 | force per unit positive charge |
| electric field strength | B1+B1 | force per unit charge + exerted on a positive charge / placed at a point |
| electric potential | M1+A1 | work done per unit charge + moving a positive charge from infinity |
| capacitance | M1+A1 | charge per unit potential difference + charge on one plate / p.d. across plates |

## 计算题模式

| 公式 | 标记 | 典型代入 |
|------|------|---------|
| $E = Q / (4\pi \epsilon_0 r^2)$ | C1+A1 | 注意 $r$ 从中心算起 |
| $V = Q / (4\pi \epsilon_0 r)$ | C1+A1 | 代数叠加 |
| $F = Q_1 Q_2 / (4\pi \epsilon_0 r^2)$ | C1+C1+A1 | 代入数值 + 8.99 × 10$^9$ |
| $E = V/d$ | C1+A1 | 单位转换 |
| $\frac{1}{2} mv^2 = qV$ | C1+C1+A1 | 能量守恒求速度 |

## 对比题模式

| 对比 | 标记 | 要点 |
|------|------|------|
| similar | B1 | both inverse square law / both radial / both $1/r^2$ |
| different | B1 | electric can be attractive or repulsive / gravitational only attractive |
| compare potentials | B1 | both $\propto 1/r$ / same sign or opposite sign |

## 作图题模式

| 图形 | 标记 | 要点 |
|------|------|------|
| field lines between plates | B1+B1+B1 | parallel + equally spaced + arrows + straight |
| $E$-$r$ for sphere | B1+B1+B1 | $E = 0$ inside + $E \propto 1/r^2$ outside + starts at $E_0$ |
| path of particle | B1+B1 | curved (parabolic) + straight after field |
| $V$-$r$ for sphere | B1+B1+B1 | constant inside + $V \propto 1/r$ outside |

## 关键词速查

| 关键词 | 含义 |
|--------|------|
| force per unit positive charge | 电场强度定义 |
| work done per unit charge | 电势定义 |
| from infinity | 电势参考点 |
| inverse square law | 反平方定律 |
| radial field | 径向场 |
| uniform field | 匀强场 |
| potential gradient | 电势梯度 |
| $E = -dV/dr$ | 场强与电势关系 |
