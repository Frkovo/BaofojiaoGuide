# 考前速记 Last Minute Summary

## 核心公式 Key Formulas

$$F = BIL\sin\theta \quad \text{(载流导体)}$$
$$F = BQv\sin\theta \quad \text{(运动电荷)}$$
$$V_H = \frac{BI}{ntq} \quad \text{(Hall voltage)}$$
$$\Phi = BA \quad \text{(magnetic flux)}$$
$$E = \frac{\Delta(N\Phi)}{\Delta t} \quad \text{(Faraday's law)}$$

## 考前 Checklist

- [ ] Tesla 定义: "newton per ampere per metre"
- [ ] Magnetic flux: "product of flux density and cross-sectional area perpendicular to the field"
- [ ] Lenz's law: "direction of induced e.m.f. opposes the change producing it"
- [ ] Faraday's law: "magnitude of induced e.m.f. proportional to rate of change of flux linkage"
- [ ] 单位转换: 1 cm = $10^{-2}$ m, 1 mm = $10^{-3}$ m
- [ ] 线圈面积: $A = \pi r^2$, 用半径不是直径
- [ ] 磁场线图: 长直导线 → 同心圆，间距增大，注意方向
- [ ] 涡电流解释: 先有变化磁场 → e.m.f. → eddy current → force
