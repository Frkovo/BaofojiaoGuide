# 评分标准分析 — Ideal Gases

## 常见 Command Words 及得分要点

### "State the basic assumptions of the kinetic theory"
- 每个假设 **B1**（通常写3-4个）
  - molecules in (continuous) random motion
  - (all) collisions are (perfectly) elastic
  - no intermolecular forces (except during collisions)
  - volume of molecules negligible compared with volume of gas
  - collisions are instantaneous

### "Explain how molecular movement causes pressure"
- **B1**: molecules collide with (walls of) container
- **B1**: momentum of molecule changes during collision (with walls)
- **B1**: change in momentum causes force on wall by molecule
- **B1**: many molecules exerting force across area leads to pressure

### "Use pV = NkT"
- **C1**: 写出公式
- **C1**: 正确代入
- **A1**: 答案

## 常见推导评分
推导 $\frac12 m\langle c^2\rangle = \frac32 kT$：
- **M1**: 联立 $pV = \frac13 Nm\langle c^2\rangle$ 和 $pV = NkT$
- **A1**: $NkT = \frac13 Nm\langle c^2\rangle$ → $\frac12 m\langle c^2\rangle = \frac32 kT$
