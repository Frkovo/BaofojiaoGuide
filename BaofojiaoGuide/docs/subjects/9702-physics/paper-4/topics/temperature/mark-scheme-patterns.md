# 评分标准分析 — Temperature

## 常见 Command Words 及得分要点

### "Define specific heat capacity"
- **M1**: energy/thermal energy per unit mass
- **A1**: per unit (temperature) change / per K / per °C

### "Define specific latent heat"
- **M1**: energy/thermal energy per unit mass
- **A1**: (during change of state / phase change) / without change of temperature

### "State examples of thermometric properties"
- **B2** each: density of liquid, volume of gas at constant pressure, resistance of metal, e.m.f. of thermocouple

## 典型 MS 表达

| 表达 | 得分 |
|------|------|
| "thermal energy transferred from hot to cold" | **B1** |
| "regions of equal temperature are in thermal equilibrium" | **B1** |
| "energy/heat per unit mass per unit temperature change" | **M1**, **A1** |
| "temperature in Kelvin = temperature in °C + 273" | **B1** |

## 计算题评分要点
- **C1**: 写出公式 $Q = mc\Delta\theta$
- **C1**: 正确代入
- **A1**: 答案正确
- 热量守恒题必须有 $Q_{\text{lost}} = Q_{\text{gained}}$
