# 评分标准分析 — Motion in a Circle

## 常见 Command Words 及得分要点

### "Define the radian"
- 角度(angle)对应的弧长(arc length)等于半径(radius)时，该角度为 1 弧度
- **B1**: 必须有 arc length = radius

### "Show that" / "Calculate" 向心力
- **C1**: 写出公式 $F = mr\omega^2$ 或 $F = mv^2/r$
- **C1**: 代入数值
- **A1**: 正确答案，含单位

### "Explain why centripetal force is needed"
- **B1**: (resultant) force is perpendicular to velocity/direction of motion
- **B1**: force causes change in direction (not speed)
- **B1**: force directed towards centre of circle

## 典型 MS 表达

| 表达 | 得分 |
|------|------|
| "(resultant) force provides the centripetal force" | **B1** |
| "(all) forces are equal and opposite" (平衡时) | **B1** |
| "force perpendicular to velocity / direction of motion" | **B1** |
| "acceleration towards centre of circle" | **B1** |

## Error Carried Forward (ECF)
- 只要后续计算与前面错误一致，ECF 适用
- 物理方程错误(XP)不适用 ECF
