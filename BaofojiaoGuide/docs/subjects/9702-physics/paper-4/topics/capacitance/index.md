# Capacitance

## 考纲要求

- 19.1 Capacitors and capacitance
  - Define capacitance (including isolated spherical conductors and parallel plate capacitors)
  - Use $C = Q / V$
  - Derive and use formulae for capacitors in series and parallel
- 19.2 Energy stored in a capacitor
  - Determine energy from area under $V$-$Q$ graph
  - Use $W = \frac{1}{2} QV = \frac{1}{2} CV^2$
- 19.3 Discharging a capacitor
  - Analyse graphs of $V$, $Q$, $I$ against $t$ for RC discharge
  - Use time constant $\tau = RC$
  - Use $x = x_0 e^{-t/RC}$ for $I$, $Q$ or $V$ during discharge

## 常见题型

- 定义题：Define capacitance
- 计算题：Combined capacitance / RC discharge / Energy stored
- 作图题：Sketch $Q$-$t$, $I$-$t$, $V$-$t$ 放电曲线
- 解释题：Explain the function of capacitors in circuits

## 核心公式

$$C = \frac{Q}{V}$$

$$\frac{1}{C} = \frac{1}{C_1} + \frac{1}{C_2} \quad \text{(series)}$$

$$C = C_1 + C_2 \quad \text{(parallel)}$$

$$W = \frac{1}{2} QV = \frac{1}{2} CV^2 = \frac{1}{2} \frac{Q^2}{C}$$

$$\tau = RC$$

$$x = x_0 e^{-t/RC}$$

## 常见错误

- 电容串并联公式与电阻搞反
- 放电用 $x = x_0(1 - e^{-t/RC})$（这是充电公式）
- 时间常数 $\tau = RC$ 的单位是 s（不是 $\Omega$F 等奇怪单位）
