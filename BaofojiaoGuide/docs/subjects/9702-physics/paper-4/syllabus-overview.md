# 考纲总览

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 试卷编号 | Paper 4 — A Level Structured Questions |
| 考试时长 | 2 小时 |

## 前置知识要求

Paper 4 假设考生已掌握 AS Level 内容（Topic 1–11），包括：
- Physical quantities and units
- Kinematics (SUVAT equations, $s = ut + \frac12 at^2$, $v^2 = u^2 + 2as$)
- Dynamics (Newton's laws, momentum, $F = ma$)
- Forces, density and pressure ($\Delta p = \rho g \Delta h$, upthrust $F = \rho gV$)
- Work, energy and power
- Deformation of solids
- Waves (Doppler effect $f_o = f_s \frac{v}{v \pm v_s}$)
- Superposition
- Electricity (current $I = Anvq$, series/parallel resistors, $P = IV$)
- DC circuits
- Particle physics
