## 考纲要求

- Identify independent variable (IV), dependent variable (DV), and control variables (CVs) [2 marks]
- Describe method to vary IV and measure DV [5 marks]
- Draw labelled diagram of workable experiment
- Describe method of analysis [3 marks]
- Additional detail including safety [6 marks]

## 常见题型

- [Mechanics Experiments](./question-types.md#question-type-1-spring-experiments) — springs, motion, forces
- [Electrical Experiments](./question-types.md#question-type-2-electrical-circuit-experiments) — circuits, capacitors, coils
- [Thermal Experiments](./question-types.md#question-type-3-thermal-experiments) — heating, cooling
- [Wave Experiments](./question-types.md#question-type-4-wave-experiments) — standing waves, sound

## 核心公式

Linearization is key:

- $y = ax^n \rightarrow \lg y = \lg a + n \lg x$
- $y = ae^{kx} \rightarrow \ln y = \ln a + kx$
- $y = \frac{a}{x} \rightarrow y = a \cdot \frac{1}{x}$

## 常见错误

- 混淆 IV 和 DV（看题目中 "how $Y$ varies with $X$" → $X$ = IV, $Y$ = DV）
- 控制变量不具体
- 忘记重复测量
