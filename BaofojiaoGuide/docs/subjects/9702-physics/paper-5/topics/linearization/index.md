## 考纲要求

- Understand how a graph of $y$ against $x$ is used to find constants $m$ and $c$ in $y=mx+c$
- Understand how $\lg y$ against $\lg x$ finds constants in $y=ax^n$
- Understand how $\ln y$ against $x$ finds constants in $y=ae^{kx}$
- Decide what derived quantities to calculate from raw data

## 常见题型

- [Standard linearization](./question-types.md#question-type-1-standard-linearization-y--mx--c) — simple rearrangement
- [Log-log linearization](./question-types.md#question-type-2-log-log-linearization-lg-y--lg-a--n-lg-x) — power laws
- [Log-linear linearization](./question-types.md#question-type-3-log-linear-linearization-ln-y--ln-a--kx) — exponential relationships
- [Reciprocal linearization](./question-types.md) — inverse relationships

## 核心公式

- $y = mx + c$: plot $y$ vs $x$, gradient $= m$, intercept $= c$
- $y = ax^n$: plot $\lg y$ vs $\lg x$, gradient $= n$, intercept $= \lg a$
- $y = ae^{kx}$: plot $\ln y$ vs $x$, gradient $= k$, intercept $= \ln a$

## 常见错误

- Choosing wrong axes
- Forgetting units in logarithms: $\ln(V/\text{V})$, not $\ln V$
- Mixing $\ln$ and $\lg$
