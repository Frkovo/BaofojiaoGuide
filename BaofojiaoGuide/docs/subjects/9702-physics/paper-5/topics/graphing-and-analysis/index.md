## 考纲要求

- Plot a graph following conventions required for Paper 3
- Show error bars in both directions
- Draw straight line of best fit and worst acceptable line
- Determine gradient and y-intercept
- Estimate absolute uncertainty in gradient and y-intercept

## 核心公式

- $\text{gradient} = \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}$
- $\text{uncertainty in gradient} = |\text{best gradient} - \text{worst gradient}|$
- $y\text{-intercept}$: substitute $(x, y)$ and gradient into $y = mx + c$

## 常见错误

- Points not accurate to half a small square
- Error bars asymmetrical or missing
- WAL doesn't go through all error bars
- Points too close for gradient calculation
