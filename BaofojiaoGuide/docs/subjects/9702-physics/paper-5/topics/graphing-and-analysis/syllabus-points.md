# 考纲要点

## Q2 作图与分析考纲要求

Paper 5 Q2 测试考生处理实验数据的能力，包括作图、误差分析和从图中求常数。

### Part (c)(i): Plotting the Graph（2 分）

- **M1**: All points plotted correctly within half a small square
- **M1**: Error bars plotted correctly in Y-direction, symmetrical, all points

### Part (c)(ii): Drawing Lines（2 分）

- **M1**: Straight line of best fit drawn (equal distribution of points above and below)
- **M1**: Worst acceptable straight line drawn (steepest or shallowest through all error bars)

### Part (c)(iii): Gradient Determination（2 分）

- **M1**: Correct gradient of best fit line
- **M1**: Correct absolute uncertainty in gradient

### Part (c)(iv): y-intercept Determination（1–2 分）

- **M1**: Correct y-intercept with unit
- **M1**: Correct uncertainty in y-intercept (if required)

## 评分总览

| Part | 内容 | 分值 |
|------|------|------|
| (c)(i) | Plot points + error bars | 2 |
| (c)(ii) | Best fit + worst acceptable lines | 2 |
| (c)(iii) | Gradient + uncertainty | 2 |
| (c)(iv) | y-intercept (+ uncertainty) | 1–2 |
