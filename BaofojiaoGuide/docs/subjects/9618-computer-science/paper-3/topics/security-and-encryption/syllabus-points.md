## 17 Security and Encryption

Candidates should be able to:

- **17.1** Show understanding of encryption:
  - symmetric encryption (private key)
  - asymmetric encryption (public / private key pair)
  - the use of a shared key
- **17.2** Show understanding of SSL / TLS:
  - purpose of SSL/TLS
  - the SSL/TLS handshake process
  - how SSL/TLS provides secure communication
- **17.3** Show understanding of digital certificates:
  - purpose of digital certificates
  - how digital certificates are acquired (Certificate Authority)
  - information contained in a digital certificate
  - how digital certificates are used to verify identity
- **17.4** Show understanding of digital signatures:
  - purpose of digital signatures
  - how digital signatures are created and verified
  - ensuring integrity and non-repudiation
- **17.5** Show understanding of quantum cryptography:
  - basic principles
  - benefits and limitations compared to classical cryptography
